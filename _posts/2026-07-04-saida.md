Saida — Sidon — is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world,
and it's home. This is a slow, growing archive of the city: its sea castle, its
souks, its rooftops and shoreline, across old photographs and new.

I've been collecting these images over the last while from local **Facebook
groups** dedicated to Saida. **I don't hold the copyright to them** — they belong
to the photographers and residents who shared them. I'm gathering them here out of
love for the city, as a living scrapbook I'll keep adding to. If one of these is
yours and you'd like it credited or removed, just email me.
