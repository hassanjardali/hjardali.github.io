We introduce a minimalistic autonomous racing stack for high-speed time-trial racing that emphasizes rapid deployment and efficient system integration with minimal on-track testing. Implemented as a C++/ROS2-based, safety-critical autonomy stack, the system reached a top speed of 206 km/h within just 11 hours of practice, covering 325 km on track.
