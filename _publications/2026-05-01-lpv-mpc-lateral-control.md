We present a Linear Parameter-Varying Model Predictive Control (LPV-MPC) formulation for the lateral control of a full-scale autonomous race car. By linearizing the vehicle dynamics around varying operating points, the controller keeps the car accurately on its racing line at high speed while remaining fast enough to run in real time on-board.
